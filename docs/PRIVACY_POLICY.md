# Privacy Policy for PingMe

**Last Updated**: November 21, 2025

## Introduction

PingMe ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, department, role (student/faculty)
- **Profile Data**: User preferences and settings
- **Focus Sessions**: Duration, timestamps, distraction counts
- **Achievements**: Progress and unlocked badges

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: App usage statistics, feature interactions
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Performance Data**: Crash reports, error logs

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Provide Services**: Enable focus mode, track statistics, manage achievements
2. **Improve Experience**: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, enhance features
3. **Communication**: Send notifications about focus sessions and achievements
4. **Security**: Protect against unauthorized access and fraudulent activity

## Data Storage

- All data is stored securely in Firebase Firestore
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- We implement industry-standard security measures

## Data Sharing

We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share data with third parties for marketing
- Use your data for advertising

We MAY share data:
- With faculty (for students in their department only)
- When required by law
- To protect our rights and safety

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
- Opt-out of notifications

## Children's Privacy

PingMe is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely
- Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days
- Backups: Retained for 90 days

## Third-Party Services

We use:
- **Firebase**: Authentication, database, analytics
- **Google Play Services**: App distribution

Please review their privacy policies.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes via the app.
