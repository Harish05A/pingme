## Getting Started

### 1. Sign Up

1. Open the PingMe app
2. Tap **"Sign Up"**
3. Choose your role:
   - **Student**: For personal focus tracking
   - **Faculty**: For monitoring student progress
4. Enter your details:
   - Full Name
   - Email
   - Password (min 6 characters)
   - Department
5. Tap **"Create Account"**

### 2. Sign In

1. Enter your email and password
2. Tap **"Sign In"**
3. You'll be directed to your dashboard

### Starting a Focus Session

1. **Select Duration**:
   - Choose a preset: Pomodoro (25min), Deep Work (90min)
   - Or enter custom duration (1-180 minutes)

2. **Get Motivated**:
   - Read the motivational quote

   - Tap the large **"Start Focus"** button

### During a Focus Session

- **Pause**: Tap pause button if needed
- **Resume**: Tap play button to continue
- **End Early**: Tap stop button (counts as incomplete)

### App Blocking

When you try to open distracting apps (Instagram, YouTube, etc.):
- A blocking overlay appears
- Tap **"Return to Focus"** to go back
- Distraction counter increments

## Troubleshooting ğŸ”§

### App Blocking Not Working

1. Grant overlay permission
2. Enable accessibility service
3. Restart app

### Notifications Not Showing

1. Check notification permissions
2. Enable "Exact Alarms" (Android 12+)
3. Disable battery optimization for PingMe

### Streak Not Updating

1. Ensure session was successful
2. Check internet connection
3. Pull to refresh on home screen

### Statistics Not Loading

1. Check internet connection
2. Pull down to refresh
3. Sign out and sign in again

**Q: Can I pause a focus session?**
